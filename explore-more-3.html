About the Magazine

CovetED is a distinguished magazine that focuses on luxury lifestyle, design, and high-end interiors. Launched with the intent to inspire and inform, Coveted covers a wide array of topics including exclusive interviews with top designers, the latest trends in home décor, and profiles of some of the most stunning residences and hotels around the world. Its pages are filled with exquisite photography and detailed articles that cater to readers with a taste for the finer things in life. Coveted has established itself as a must-read for those seeking inspiration in the realms of opulence and sophisticated living.
